4The Epicycle Engine

The blue bars below are |poly[zk]| at the current t — the magnitudes of polynomial coefficients computed by multiplying one factor (1 + z·wx + z2·w2x + ⋯) per distinct list value. The gray z0 bar is excluded by the size constraint (size ≥ 1). The teal line traces the running magnitude |poly[z1] + ⋯ + poly[zk]|, ending at |f(t)| on the right — the same value appears in the detector canvas below as √(X² + Y²) of the chain tip's coordinates.
